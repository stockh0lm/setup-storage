FAI - Fully Automatic Installation

FAI 3.4.x

The new version 3.4.8 and newer support Debian 6.0 (aka squeeze) out of the box. It can also be used with Debian 5.0 (aka lenny) with some minor changes.
  • Entry for /etc/apt/sources.list and for /etc/fai/apt/sources.list: 

    deb http://fai-project.org/download squeeze koeln

 

Packages for Ubuntu

FAI 3.4.x packages are included in Ubuntu maverick (10.10) 

deb http://archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu maverick universe

Currently they are backported, so they will also be included in lucid (10.04 LTS).
